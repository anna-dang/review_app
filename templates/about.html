About

This app is a demonstration of the classification model I built for my final project in Flatiron's Data Science Bootcamp. Please view my full analysis and my presentation.

Objective

For the hospitality industry, reputation is everything. Guests book based on online presence and word-of-mouth. Negative reviews can largely impact the booking decisions of future guests. For negative guest experiences, time and care are crucial for guest recovery. Finding and attending to an unhappy guest before they post a review can change their experience, and their score.

Business Problem - Reach dissatisfied guests quickly to increase customer retention.

Business Solution - Build a communication management tool to flag dissatisfied guests for rapid recovery by staff.

How to Use

This app is a demonstration of how the communication tool will perform. Input a hotel review on the main page and click 'Classify'. The app will return a 'flag' or 'pass' status. Meaning: whether the review is 'flagged' for immediate guest recovery or if it 'passes' and can be left for regular attention. The app also returns a predicted numerical rating.
